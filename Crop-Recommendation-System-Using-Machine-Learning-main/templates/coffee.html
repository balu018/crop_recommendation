COFFEE

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain Coffea species.

The flavor of the coffee depends on the roasting process and the variety of coffee bean used.

Coffee is one of the most popular beverages in the world, enjoyed by millions of people every day.

It contains caffeine, a natural stimulant that can help improve focus, alertness, and mood.

Coffee cultivation is an important industry in many countries, providing employment and economic opportunities.

The process of growing and harvesting coffee beans involves several stages, from planting and pruning to harvesting and processing.
