COTTON

Cotton is a soft, fluffy staple fiber that grows in a boll, or protective case, around the seeds of cotton plants.

It is used in a variety of products including clothing, textiles, and medical supplies.

Cotton is a highly versatile crop that can be grown in a variety of climates and soil types.

It is also relatively easy to grow, making it a popular choice for farmers.

Cotton is a sustainable crop that requires minimal water and pesticides to grow.

It is also biodegradable, making it an environmentally friendly choice.
